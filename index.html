Client Delivery Date

Contractor Due Date + a revision buffer sized to the project's total deliverable count.

  • 20+ deliverables → 4–5 day revision window.
  • ~5 deliverables → 2–3 day revision window.
  • Everything in between scales accordingly (built into the matrix below).
